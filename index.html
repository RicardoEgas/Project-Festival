"Hello! Sharing World!"

The Event Listener Music Festival 2015

A joyful celebration believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries is taking place in October, in Korea.

2015.10.15(THU)~16(FRI)

@Gwangalli Beach, Busan

Including

  • Music
  • We made history by discovering new musical promises and presenting the most renowned names in music worldwide. With a careful and coherent lineup, come join us in this adventure and share the message of music.
  • Camping
  • Camping is the ultimate festival experience. You can camp for free or if you want a bit more comfort book your spot in our upgrade campsites with accommodations!
  • Food & drinks
  • We will have over 400 food traders offering an array of exciting eats for Festival-goers. The choices are endless with a variety of delicious, world cuisine available across the site.
  • Chill zone
  • The area is meant as a quality chill-out zone. There will be cool daytime concerts and dj-sets during the afternoon and night, along with a bar and smoothie corner.
  • Traders
  • Thousands of food traders, charities, campaigning organisations and sellers of the bizarre and unique apply to trade at our site each year
